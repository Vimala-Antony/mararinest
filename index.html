About Our Homestay

Welcome to Mararinest Beach Homestay, a family-run retreat just steps away from the golden sands of Marari Beach. Here, every guest is treated like a part of our family, with genuine care and warm hospitality that make your stay truly special.

Wake up to the rhythm of the waves, enjoy the comfort of our cozy rooms, and savor home-cooked Kerala delicacies prepared with love. Surrounded by coconut palms and the refreshing sea breeze, Mararinest offers a perfect escape for couples, families, and solo travelers alike.
